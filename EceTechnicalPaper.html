Technical Paper

Whether experienced at writing papers or just beginning, it is always useful to have your memory refreshed on what constitutes a successful technical paper. Clearly, a successful paper is one that is accepted into a technical publication and then is read and referenced by others. To achieve this end, it must first be determined that a particular body of work is unique and valuable to others. Second, the paper must be well written and follow the style guide of the chosen publication. This article covers the basics of paper acceptance, and reviews many of the writing pitfalls made by both veteran and beginner authors alike.

Rules

    1. Maximum number of participants in a team is 2.
    2. The topics that the papers need to be based on are suggestive. But the participants have to abide by the themes provided below.
    3. Abstract should not exceed 1 page and paper should not exceed 10 pages.
    4. Presentation of paper will be held on 12th October 2017.
    5. Bring college i-card on the day of the event.
    6. Soft copies of the submitted paper should be in .docx format.
    7. Please mail your abstract, paper and presentation to tpptechapratim@gmail.com. Difficulties will be addressed through the same email-id.
    8. Please bring 2 hard copies of the submitted paper on the day of the event.
    9. The teams will get 10 minutes for presentation followed by question and answer session.
    10. Please bring the power point presentation in a cd as well as a pendrive on the day of the presentation.
    11. One person cannot be a part of multiple teams for the same event and both the team members must be from the same institution.
    12. The mail with submission should contain :
    Title- theme of the paper
    Name of both the authors
    Phone no. of both the authors
    Email id of both the authors
    13. Paper should be in one column format and should not exceed 10 pages
    14. Paper should be in the Times New Roman font of size 12.
    15. For heading use bold and a font of size 16.
    16. Paper must contain index, list of tables, abstract, introduction, point wise description of subject, conclusion and references.
    17. Paper must be preceded by a cover page specifying the title of the paper, names of authors and their college names as well as their contact number and email id.
    18. Last day to submit soft copy of the paper (via e mail) will be 5days prior to the day of the presentation.
    19. After submission of the soft copies, the teams will be shortlisted and notified to give a presentation.
    20. In case of disagreement the decision of the judges will be final and no arguments or appeals will be entertained.
    21. The winners of the competition will be provided with a certificate from IEEE.
