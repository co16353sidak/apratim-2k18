Mech Quiz

About Mech Quiz

The Mechanical Quiz at Mech-Tech Apratim is a quiz competition based on Mechanical Engineering with specific focus on Automobile Engineering. It is open for single person entry only.

Mech Quiz

Rules

Round 1

  1. Round 1 will be an elimination round.
  2. An MCQ quiz with 20 questions will be given.
  3. Time allotted for the first round will be 15 minutes.
  4. There will be no negative marking.
  5. Top 6 scorers will advance to the next round.

Round 2

  1. Round 2 will be an Audio/Visual round.
  2. Each question carries +3 points for a right answer and -1 for a wrong one.
  3. Passed questions will have +1 points and no negative points.
  4. Each individual will have 3 questions and 45 seconds to answer each question.

Round 3

  1. Round 3 will be a rapid fire round.
  2. 8 questions with one point each will be asked in a span of one minute.
  3. No negative points for this round.


  • The person with the highest cumulative score in round 2 and 3 will be declared as the winner along with first and second runner up.
  • Individual entries only.
  • Certificates will be given to all the participants.
  • The organizers reserve all rights to change any of the above rules as they deem fit. Changes in rules, if any will be highlighted on this page.
  • In case of any discrepancy decision of the judges shall be treated as final and binding on all.
