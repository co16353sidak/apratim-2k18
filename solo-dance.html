Solo-Dance

Dance is the hidden language of the soul of the body.

Dance is a performing art form consisting of purposefully selected sequences of human movement. This movement has aesthetic and symbolic value, and is acknowledged as dance by performers and observers within a particular culture.

Solo Dance

Competition Structure

  1. Prelims
  2. Finals

Rules-Prelims

  1. Track limit : 2-3 minutes.
  2. No marks for props and costumes.
  3. Stunts related to fire and water are not allowed.
  4. All Dance Forms are allowed
  5. Participants should bring their music track in pen drives as well as CDs.
  6. Vulgarity of any kind will lead to immediate disqualification.
  7. Registrations can be done online and on the spot.
  8. The decision of the judges will be final.

Rules-Finals

  1. Track limit : 3-4minutes.
  2. All dance Forms are allowed.
  3. Stunts related to fire and water are not allowed.
  4. Participants should bring their music track in pen drives as well as CDs.
  5. The decision of the judges will be final.
