Skit

About Skit

Applause is the spur of noble minds, the end and aim of weak ones.

Skit are an excellent way for new actors start out. Rather than giving them a full play give them something short to work with and play with. These skits may also be used for Reader's Theatre in the classroom as a quick and easy lesson. Use the short play as an introduction to get students interested in a topic or as a fun way to get students up and moving or You can create an entire evening of entertainment in the style of Saturday Night Live by using several sketches.

Street Play

Rules

  1. Script should be original/self-written and not be copied, if found guilty, would result in disqualification of team.
  2. Team limit is 20-25 members including member in charge of music (There is no limitation on minimum number of members).
  3. No restriction on use of musical instruments. Marks will be on creative use of musical instruments and not on their number.
  4. No limit on the number of props used. Marks will be given on creative use of props and not on their number.
  5. Vulgarity, blasphemy, offensive content against any particular sect/ public figure is strictly not permissible; points may be deducted for the same.
  6. Language used can be Hindi or English.
  7. Any electronic device or recorded music is not allowed.
  8. Negative points for exceeding time limit.
  9. Decision of judges will be final.
  10. Time Limit: 15-25 min.
