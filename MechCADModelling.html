CAD Modelling

The CAD Contest at Mech-Tech Apratim is a mechanical design competition focusing on 3-D modelling of various mechanical parts. It is open for single person entry only. The contestants will be assigned various challenging models with enough dimensions and 2D views to create the 3-D model of the same.

CAD Modelling

Rules


    1. The total time given for the modelling of parts is 2hrs.
    2. Participants must bring their own laptops for the competition, as laptops will not be provided at the competition. They are allowed to use any 3D modeling software (2D drafting software are not allowed).
    3. Drawings for a total of 6 parts with varying difficulty level will be provided.
    4. The marks will be allotted to each part according to their difficulty level.
    5. The participants has to model as many 3d parts as possible and as accurately as possible in the time allotted to them.
    6. The judging will be done by matching the volume of the 3d model generated by the participants to the original CAD model from which the drawings are being made.
    7. In case the volume of a 3d model matches to that of the original CAD model within a percentage error of 2% the full marks will be provided to that participant.
    8. In case the volume of a 3d model does not matches to that of the original CAD model within a percentage error of 2% the marks will be deducted after inspection of the model.
    9. In case any participant fails to complete the CAD model within the modelling time provided, that model will be disqualified and will not be judged.
    10. The person with the highest score in the end will be declared as the winner along with first and second runner up.
    11. Individual entries only.
    Certificates will be given to all the participants.
    The organizers reserve all rights to change any of the above rules as they deem fit. Changes in rules, if any will be highlighted on the website.
    In case of a discrepancy decision of the judges shall be treated as final and binding on all.
